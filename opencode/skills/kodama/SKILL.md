## Time Horizons

| Horizon | Use |
|---|---|
| < 3 months | Out of scope — surface-optimization workflows handle this |
| 3-6 months | Recommended for early-stage gap closure and quick proof wins |
| 6-9 months | Recommended for certification preparation or larger project builds |
| > 9 months | Accepted but note uncertainty increases; reassess at 9 months |

Default: 6 months when the user does not specify.

## GROW-02 Certificate Gate

Certificate recommendations require ALL three conditions:
1. Same gap recurs across ≥2 role-fit snapshots with the same blocker label
2. The domain is market-rewarded (cloud architecture, AWS/Azure/GCP, Kubernetes, security, ML, data engineering, PMP)
3. The certificate materially beats project/proof alternatives — a portfolio cannot replicate the breadth, depth, or market-standard signal

If ANY condition is not met, do not recommend a certificate. Use project/proof instead.

## Project/Proof-First Framing

Always present project/proof recommendations before certificate content. Frame each project recommendation with:
- The specific gap it addresses
- Why a project is the right vehicle for this gap
- Scope and suggested timeline

## What Not to Pursue

Explicitly list low-leverage actions. Frame as focus-preserving guidance: "These actions would dilute effort without commensurate signal gain in the medium horizon." Connect to the user's role target and constraints.

## Recurring Gap Detection

A gap is recurring when the same blocker label appears in ≥2 role-fit snapshots for the same role family. Single-snapshot gaps are not recurring — do not treat them as patterns.

## Advisory Posture

Do not enroll users in courses, modify profiles, or publish growth plans. All recommendations are for user action. The recommended_next_action should be the single most impactful step the user can take today.
